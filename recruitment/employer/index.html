How The Program Works

We’ve built a structured process to make international hiring simple, efficient, and tailored to your needs.

1. We Start
With You

We begin by listening to you. We conduct an interview to understand the roles you're looking to fill, the challenges with your current team, and the key skills that matter most to your success.

This allows us to identify and present only candidates who are genuinely aligned with your business.

2. We Assess
Visa Feasibility

Not sure if you can sponsor a foreign worker? We help you evaluate whether a U.S. work visa (J-1, H-1B, O-1, etc.) is a viable option for your company.

You'll have the support of two specialized law firms, one based in Miami and one in Italy, working in synergy to offer you complete and personalized legal guidance throughout the process.

3. We Select The
Right Candidates

We collaborate with some of the top Italian culinary and hospitality schools to identify motivated, well-trained professionals between 18 and 40 years old who are eager to work in the United States.

We carefully evaluate both hard and soft skills, as well as English proficiency (and Spanish, if required).
